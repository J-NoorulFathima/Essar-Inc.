01 / The Local Node

Activating specialized regional creators where your product lives.

The Mechanics:

Zero-Aviation Framework: Instead of flying a director and crew from a metro hub (Mumbai/Delhi) to your location, we engage pre-vetted, elite local creators based in your region for in-situ shoot.

Native Lens & Context: These local creators bring deep regional familiarity, capturing authentic visual narratives that an outside crew would spend days trying to understand.

Standardized Capture Protocols: To maintain cinematic consistency, every local creator operates under strict Essar Inc. technical guidelines (resolution, color space, audio bit-rates and picture boards).

02 / The Tier 3 Cloud Node

Centralizing post-production through secure, high-performance networks.

The Mechanics:

Direct Cloud Ingestion: The moment the shoot wraps, raw footage is not copied to physical hard drives and shipped via courier. It is securely ingested into our localized cloud storage nodes.

Collaborative Remote Editing: Our network of expert editors, colorists and sound designers access the proxy assets remotely, working in real-time from optimised, energy-efficient digital workspaces.

Virtual Rendering Hubs: Final rendering is executed on specialised cloud servers optimised for energy efficiency, utilizing virtual processing power rather than carbon-heavy physical studio hardware running 24/7.
